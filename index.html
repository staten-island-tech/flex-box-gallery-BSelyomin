Minecraft Wood

All 9 Types of Minecraft Wood

Oak

The oak log is a naturally generated block that can be found in plains, swamps, oak forests, rivers, jungle variants, savannahs, wooded badlands, wooded mountains, and dark forests, plains and meadows villages, and mineshafts make it so it can spawn in any other biome. Oak logs are mainly used for crafting spruce planks or decoration.

Spruce

The spruce log is a naturally generated block that is mostly found in the taiga biome but can also be found in windswept forests, snowy plains, snowy taiga, and old growth taiga biomes, as well as in taiga and snowy villages. Spruce logs are mainly used for crafting spruce planks or decoration.

Birch

The birch log is a naturally generated block that can be found in forests, hills, plains and in birch forests. Birch logs are mainly used for crafting birch planks or decoration.

Jungle

The jungle log is a naturally generated block that can only be found in jungle forests. Jungle logs are mainly used for crafting jungle planks or decoration.

Acacia

The acacia log is a naturally generated block that can be found in savannah biome and savannah villages. Acacia logs are mainly used for crafting acacia planks or decoration.

Dark Oak

The dark oak log is a naturally generated block that can only be found in the dark forest biome. Dark oak logs are mainly used for crafting dark oak planks or decoration.

Crimson

The crimson stem a naturally generated block that can only be found in the crimson forest biome of the nether. Crimson stems are mainly used for crafting crimson planks or decoration.

Warped

The warped stem is a naturally generated block that can only be found in the warped forests biome of the nether. Warped stems are mainly used for crafting warped planks or decoration.

Mangrove

The mangrove log is a naturally generated block that can only be found in the mangrove swamps biome. Mangrove logs are mainly used for crafting mangrove planks or decoration.
